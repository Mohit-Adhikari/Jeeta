Preface

What you are seeing are words emulated through codes. Hopefully, these words will bring forth the revelation of our deceitful and yet truthful minds. But for me, this is a reminiscence of an individual who once called me her son. She was taken from me because of her own innocent brain. The same brain that persuaded her that I would be better if she isn't here with me. Miss you, Mother.

About Jeeta

Whatever the requirements for the advancement of mental health may be, Jeeta will always have its core purpose to meet those requirements. It is a library for those who are sure they've known it all. It is a stage for those who know nothing and a podium for those who've lost it all.
